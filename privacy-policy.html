Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

At Trend Kro Media, accessible from trendkromedia.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Trend Kro Media and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at trendkaromedia@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out forms on our website, schedule a strategy call, or communicate with us. This information may include:

  • Your name, email address, phone number/WhatsApp number.
  • Your business/company name, website URL, and social media handles.
  • Information about your marketing goals, budgets, and business challenges discussed during consultations.
  • Any other information you choose to provide in contact forms or inquiry fields.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our personal branding and media services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website and user experience.
  • Understand and analyze how you interact with our website.
  • Develop new growth plans, services, features, and functionalities.
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, for customer service, updates, and marketing purposes.
  • Schedule consultations and free strategy calls via WhatsApp or calendar scheduling systems.
  • Send you emails or messages regarding campaigns, media placements, or PR opportunities.

3. Log Files & Analytics

Trend Kro Media follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Trend Kro Media uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

5. Advertising & Tracking Pixels

We may use tracking tools such as Meta Pixel (Facebook & Instagram ads tracking) and Google Analytics to measure ad conversions and understand our audience better. These third-party tools use cookies and tracking technologies to collect data on user activity to serve targeted advertisements based on your interests.

6. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Trend Kro Media's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party servers (like Meta, Google, Calendly) for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

7. Data Security

We employ appropriate security measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no transmission of data over the internet or storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
