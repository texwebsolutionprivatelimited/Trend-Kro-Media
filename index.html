GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before taking the next step with Trend Kro Media.

01 Is the strategy call free?

Yes, 100% free — no hidden charges, no commitments. Our Free Strategy Call is a 30-minute personalized consultation where we analyze your brand, understand your goals, and share a customized roadmap. We believe in building value first before asking for anything in return.

02 How do I get started?

Getting started is simple. Book a Free Strategy Call through our website or reach out directly via WhatsApp. After understanding your goals, our team designs a customized growth plan. Once approved, we begin execution immediately — no long delays.

03 Do you work with personal brands?

Absolutely! Personal branding is one of our strongest specialties. We work with coaches, consultants, speakers, doctors, astrologers, educators, and industry experts to build their digital presence, grow their audience, and position them as recognized authorities in their field.

04 What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including Finance, Health & Wellness, Education, Real Estate, E-Commerce, Coaching & Consulting, Startups, Hospitality, Astrology, Legal, and more. Our strategies are tailored to your specific industry, not generic templates.

05 How does TEDx positioning work?

Our TEDx positioning service is an end-to-end process. We start by identifying your signature idea worth spreading, help craft your talk concept, build the authority profile required for TEDx selection, and manage the full application process. We have successfully positioned multiple clients on TEDx stages across India.

06 How long does PR coverage take?

Typically, press releases and media coverage placements are completed within 7–21 working days, depending on the publication and approval process. We work with a network of 50+ verified media outlets across India. You'll receive a detailed report with all live links once coverage is published.

07 Do you provide podcast opportunities?

Yes! We connect you with relevant podcasts in your niche where you can share your story, expertise, and insights. Our team handles outreach, scheduling, and talking-point preparation so you show up confident and impactful. Podcast appearances significantly boost your authority and organic reach.

Still have questions? We're happy to help.
